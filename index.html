Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Local-First Commitment: Habme is designed to respect your privacy. All your habits, schedules, history, and completions are stored strictly on your device. We do not transmit, analyze, or share your data.

1. Information We Collect

Habme is a local-first application. We do not require you to register an account, nor do we collect, process, or transmit any personal data. All data generated within the app—including habit descriptions, goal progress, and completion logs—is saved exclusively in your device's local database (via Hive local storage).

2. Use of Your Information

Your local data is used solely to power the user experience: rendering your daily lists, calculating consistency statistics (completion rates and streaks), and constructing your habit heatmap history. None of this information ever leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with third-party tracking, analytics engines, or advertisement networks. We do not capture or share usage analytics or metrics.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you remain in complete control of it. You can clear all of your data at any time by uninstalling the Habme application or clearing the application cache/data through your device settings.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be updated on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how your local data is protected.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns regarding your privacy while using Habme, please feel free to reach out to us at support@habme.app.
